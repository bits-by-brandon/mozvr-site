Sechelt

Creators
  • Josh Carpenter
  • Mr.DOOB
Tools
  • Three.js
  • Cinema 4D
Source
  • GitHub

“Sechelt” is a virtual reality visualization of an inlet near the town of Sechelt, on British Columbia’s southern coast, rendered in gradients and colors inspired by the work of renowned Canadian painter Roy Henry Vickers. It was designed and built by Josh Carpenter of Mozilla in collaboration with Mr.DOOB of Three.js. Cinema4D was used to first design the look and feel of the world, and then models, textures and camera paths were imported into a Three.js scene programmed by DOOB.
