Projects

This is our full collection of virtual reality web projects, experiments, demos, etc. To enter the VR mode of these projects, be sure to use a VR-enabled browser, like Firefox with VR and a compatible VR headset.

Sechelt

A visualization of British Columbia's coast built with three.js and Cinema 4D.

Information Diving

A visualization of data as art, inspired by the work of artist Tatiana Plakhova and built using the Vizi framework.

The Polar Sea

A documentary by DEEP that takes users to the Arctic in 360° VR video, powered by eleVR’s WebGL VR video player.

Panorama Viewer

A panorama viewer made from equirectangular images mapped to a WebGL sphere that surrounds the user.

Talk Chat Show Thing Ep 4

eleVR and Mozilla discuss the future of WebVR as the sun rises over Mozilla San Francisco.

Leap Motion Collage

Manipulating a photo collage with motion control using the Leap Motion and Leap JavaScript SDK.
